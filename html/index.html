FIFA World Cup 2014 News
The biggest scoreline in the history of theFIFA WORLD Cupcualifiers- and indeed in the history of international football-was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.
This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson,whose 13-goal haul set a new world record, wich stands to this day, for an individual player in asingle international match.
And thought the defeat earned American Samoa ignominy, so inspiring has theirsubsequient recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimend documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
